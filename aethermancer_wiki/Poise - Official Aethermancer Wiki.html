Poise

From Aethermancer Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Enemy monsters have weaknesses known as Poise. Their Poise decreases each time the monster receives damage matching the corresponding Element or Wild damage. When an enemy monster's Poise reaches 0, it becomes Staggered until the end of its next turn. Most enemy monsters have an Elemental Poise corresponding to one of the four basic Elements, but some, typically Champion Monsters and Monster Deities, have unique variations. These include Poise that can be broken by any Elemental damage, multiple Elemental Poises, or Poises that change Element each round or after a Reset Poise effect. The amount of Poise a monster has is defined by this formula:

floor(Base*(1+(Levelâˆ’1)*0.175))+Mythic

where:

  • Base is the base Poise of the monster (you can check the base Poise of a monster in their Wild Data page)
  • Level is the current Level of the monster
  • Mythic is the increase when playing on Mythic Difficulty
    • For normal monsters, this increases by 1 on Mythic
    • For Champions/Deities, this increases by 2 on Mythic


Enemy monsters will reset their Poise back to its original amount after certain conditions. The Reset Poise effect occurs after the monster's Action when:

  • The monster has not been Staggered by the time of its Action on Round 3 (up to once per combat)
  • The monster was Staggered on the previous round (each time)

Stagger

When an enemy gets Staggered, its current Action (or its next turn when Staggered during or after its Action) is cancelled, triggering the player monster's On Interrupt effects. Staggered enemies throb red and have their Poise broken. They take 50% increased damage from all sources, including debuffs and Critical Hits. Staggering enemies is required for crafting a Memento of that monster.

Reset Actions

When a monster resets its Poise, it also performs an additional effect called a Reset Action, which generates a random Aether and applies an additional effect depending on the type of Reset Action. The type of Reset Action corresponds to one of the monster's Types. Despite the name, Reset Actions are not actual Actions and therefore do not trigger On Action effects. Reset Actions can be disabled in the Settings under Accessibility. When disabled, wild monsters will only perform a regular Reset Poise.

Void Abilities

When exploring in the overworld, Void Blitz can be used near a monster pack to automatically start the battle with the targeted monster losing two Poise. If the player is on higher ground when using Void Blitz, one additional Poise is broken. A Void Parry has a similar effect to the Void Blitz when the player is ambushed by a monster. The "Void Tech Upgrade" meta-upgrade can be purchased from Alioth at Pilgrim's Rest to increase Poise Damage by 1 for each completed Biome.
