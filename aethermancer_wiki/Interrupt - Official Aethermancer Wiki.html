Interrupt

From Aethermancer Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

An enemy is Interrupted when their planned attack cannot be taken anymore. This can occur in two different scenarios:

  • The enemy monster has its Poise broken, leading to it being Staggered and unable to execute its planned attack
  • The enemy monster has the Aether that it requires for its planned attack either Purged or Stolen, thus making it fall back to using its Basic Attack.


For enemy Monsters (as it is not possible to interrupt the player's Monsters), any On Interrupt triggers only happen once during combat when the action is taken.
