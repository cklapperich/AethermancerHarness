Traits

From Aethermancer Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Learning a new trait

Traits are passive Skills from monsters that occur during combat. Some Traits are continuously active and may provide additional Modifiers, while others trigger in response to specific conditions. A few Traits can only trigger a limited number of times per combat. Each Trait is assigned with at least one Type, which determines which monsters can learn it. For instance, a Power Trait can only be learned by Power monsters.

Each monster species possesses a unique Signature Trait, which cannot be learned by other monsters or replaced. Monsters learn a new Trait every time they reach an even-numbered level. When this happens, three Traits are randomly chosen from the monster's Skill pool, and the player must select one. Some Traits have an acquisition requirement, meaning a specific conditionsuch as having at least one ally that can apply Weaknessmust be met for the Trait to appear.

In addition, Lily can replace one of a monster's existing Traits with a new one, using the same three-choice selection process. Monsters can have up to four Traits, excluding their Signature Trait. Once they reach that limit, gaining a level that would normally teach a new Trait instead gives the player the option to either gain +5 Max Health or replace one of the monster's current Traits with the newly selected Trait.

A Trait can only be learned once by a monster unless it is replaced. Multiple monsters in the party can learn the same Trait, except for Traits labeled as an Aura. A Trait labeled as an Aura can only be learned by a single monster at a time and will not appear on any other monsters in the party unless the former monster is removed or the Trait is replaced.

Maverick Traits

Maverick Traits are Traits that combine two different Types. A monster can only learn these Traits if its Type matches at least one of the required Types, and if there is a monster in the party that possesses the other required Type. A monster with both required Types can learn these Traits without needing another monster in the party.

Maverick Traits are designed to enable synergies between different monster Types. Once acquired, the Trait remains available even if there are no longer any monsters with the other required Type, unless the Trait is replaced.

All Maverick Traits are listed here.

Signature Traits

Signature Traits are Traits exclusive to each monster species. Every monster possesses a Signature Trait and always starts out with this passive Skill. Signature Traits are intended to have a strong potential to be built around and ensure each monster stands out. Some of these Traits are also designed to bring variety to each combat encounter and playthrough in the game. Shifted monsters either retain the Signature Trait of their normal variant or have a slightly altered version that fits their new Type and Element.

All Signature Traits are listed here.

Enemy-exclusive Traits

There are Traits that are exclusive to special enemies known as Monster Deities. These Traits provide the Deity with a unique mechanic that affects how the player approaches the battle.
