Aether Spring Boons

Aether Spring Boons are Boons that remain active for the rest of the run after being obtained.

Possible Boons

Water

  • Tier 1 - Purifying Boon - At the end of each combat: Cleanses 1 Corruption from each of your monsters
  • Tier 2 - Water Aether Boon - At the start of your turn: Generates 1 Water Aether.
  • Tier 3a - Water Heal Boon - At the start of your turn: Heals all of your Monsters by 4.
  • Tier 3b - Water Damage Boon - Your monsters gain +1 Water Damage.

Fire

  • Tier 1 - Equipment Boon - Equipments from Random Loot Drops have a 35% chance to have a higher tier.
  • Tier 2 - Fire Aether Boon - At the start of your turn: Generates 1 Fire Aether.
  • Tier 3a - Fire Power Boon - Every 3 turns in combat: Applies Power to your monsters.
  • Tier 3b - Fire Damage Boon - Your monsters gain +1 Fire Damage.

Wind

  • Tier 1 - Experience Boon - At the end of each combat: 25% chance that one of your monsters gains 1 extra EXP.
  • Tier 2 - Wind Aether Boon - At the start of your turn: Generates 1 Wind Aether.
  • Tier 3a - Wind Nimble Boon - Your monsters gain +5% Evasion and +5% Crit Chance.
  • Tier 3b - Wind Damage Boon - Your monsters gain +1 Wind Damage.

Earth

  • Tier 1 - Gold Boon - At the end of each combat: Gains 2 extra Gold.
  • Tier 2 - Earth Aether Boon - At the start of your turn: Generates 1 Earth Aether.
  • Tier 3a - Earth Shield Boon - At the start of your turn: Shields all of your Monsters by 2.
  • Tier 3b - Earth Damage Boon - Your monsters gain +1 Earth Damage.

Boon Generation Logic

The available Boons generated at each Aether Spring changes depending on how many prior Boons have already been received.

No Prior Boons

The Aether Spring will pick two random Elements, and give their Tier 1 Boons as options.

One Prior Boon

The previous selected boon Element will be paired with one other random Element. The Tier 2 Boons of those elements will be given as options.

Two Prior Boons

If you selected the same element for the last two Boons, then the Tier 3a and 3b Boons will be given as options.

If you selected two different elements, then the Tier 3a Boons of those two elements will be given as options.

Soul Altar Boons

Soul Altars provide a temporary Boon that will last for the next two fights. Only one of these Boons can be active at a time.

Possible Boons

Monster Type Description
Aether At the start of combat: Generates 2 Wild Aether.
Affliction Whenever an ally applies a debuff, 15% chance to apply another stack of the same debuff.
Age Allies gain [Aging] and +1 damage for every 3 stacks of Age on them.
Burn At the start of combat: Applies 3 Burn to all enemies.
Critical Allies gain 15% Crit Chance.
Dodge At the start of combat: Applies 1 Dodge to all allies.
Heal Allies gain +1 Healing.
Poison At the start of combat: Applies 3 Poison to all enemies.
Power At the start of combat: Applies 1 Power to all allies.
Purge At the start of combat: Purges 1 enemy Aether.
Regeneration At the start of combat: Applies 3 Regeneration to all allies.
Shield Allies gain +1 Shielding.
Sidekick At the start of combat: Applies 2 Sidekick to all allies.
Tank Allies gain +10 Max Health.
Terror At the start of combat: Applies 3 Terror to all enemies.
Weakness At the start of combat: Applies 3 Weakness to all enemies.
